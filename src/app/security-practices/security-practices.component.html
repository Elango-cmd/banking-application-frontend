Our Security Practices

Your security is our top priority. We use state-of-the-art encryption and fraud prevention measures to keep your data safe. Here are some tips to help you protect your account:

1. Use Strong Passwords

Always create strong, unique passwords for your accounts. A strong password should include a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Enhance your account security by enabling two-factor authentication (2FA). This adds an extra layer of protection by requiring you to verify your identity using something you know (your password) and something you have (your phone).

3. Monitor Your Account Activity

Regularly check your account for any unauthorized transactions. If you notice anything suspicious, report it immediately.

4. Be Cautious of Phishing Scams

Don't click on suspicious links or emails. Always ensure the email is from a legitimate source before entering your login details.

5. Use Secure Networks

When banking online, avoid using public Wi-Fi networks. Always use a secure, private connection when accessing your bank account.

6. Log Out After Use

Always log out of your banking session when you're done, especially if you're using a shared or public computer.

If you ever notice any suspicious activity on your account, please contact us immediately.
